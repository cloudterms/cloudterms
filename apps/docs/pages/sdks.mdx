# SDKs

We have a number of SDKs available to help you quickly integrate CloudTerms into your app.

##### **[JS SDK](./sdks/js)** for an enjoyable, type safe interaction with the CloudTerms API.

##### **[React SDK](./sdks/react)** for adding a terms agreement modal to your React app.

##### **[Next.js SDK](./sdks/nextjs)** for a seamless terms agreement experience in your Next.js app.
