# Home
 
Welcome! CloudTerms is a cloud-based platform that allows you to create, manage, and easily integrate your terms of service, privacy policy and other legal documents easily into your app.

### How it works
1. Create an application in the [dashboard](https://cloudterms.dev/dashboard).
2. Add all your apps legal documents on the terms page.
3. Create an API Key and add it to your apps `.env`.
4. Intergrate CloudTerms into your JavaScript or TypeScript app using one of our SDKs.
